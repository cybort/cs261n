Related Papers

== Censorship Analysis

"How the Great Firewall of China is Blocking Tor"
http://www.cs.kau.se/philwint/pdf/foci2012.pdf
Outline: Great Firewall of China might spoof IP addresses for the purpose of scanning Tor bridges.  Tor traffic is currently distinguishable from what is regarded as harmless traffic in China.  It is crucial to minimize the distinguishability.
